# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Fashion-forward girls looking for unique boutique pieces. They want the fun and reassurance of seeing themselves in clothes virtually before purchasing.

## Product Purpose
A luxury boutique e-commerce platform that uses AI virtual try-on to provide a "fun and reassuring" shopping experience. Success is measured by ease of use, social media virality, and customer conversion.

## Brand Personality
Luxury, Simple, Unique. Bold but refined, with a focus on "diva" aesthetics and futuristic elements like 3D and holographic effects.

## Anti-references
- Cheap-looking fast-fashion sites.
- Static, lifeless interfaces.
- "AI slop" (generic, repetitive AI-generated design patterns like purple-to-blue gradients, Inter font everywhere, and nested cards).
- Generic Amazon-style marketplaces.

## Design Principles
- **Elevated Clarity**: The steps for virtual try-on must be ultra-clear and effortless, removing all friction from the "fun" experience.
- **Dynamic Presence**: Use motion and 3D effects to ensure the site feels "alive" and premium, avoiding a static feel.
- **Social-First Aesthetics**: Every screen should be "shareable," designed with social media impact in mind to drive organic growth.
- **Luxury Restraint**: Maintain a simple, high-end feel that lets the clothes and the user's try-on result be the hero.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Prioritize high visual contrast for luxury legibility.
- Ensure 3D/motion effects have reduced-motion alternatives.
- Focus on clear, intuitive navigation for all skill levels.
